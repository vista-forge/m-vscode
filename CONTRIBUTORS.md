# Contributors

`m-vscode` is **dual-licensed**: freely under the AGPL-3.0-or-later, or under a
commercial license. Offering the commercial license requires that the maintainer
hold or control all rights in the codebase — so **outside contributions require a
Contributor License Agreement**.

**Read it first: <https://vista-forge.github.io/cla.html>**

It does **not** take away your rights to your own contribution — you keep them.
If an outside contribution were AGPL-only, the combined work could never be
licensed commercially again, for anyone; the CLA is what prevents that.

## How to sign

In the **same change as your first contribution**, append your block to the
Signatures section below:

```
Name:                    Ada Lovelace
Contact:                 ada@example.org
Entity (if applicable):  —
Date:                    2026-07-22
I accept the vista-forge CLA (https://vista-forge.github.io/cla.html): yes
```

**Contributing on behalf of an employer?** An authorized signer must accept on the
entity's behalf, by the same mechanism, naming the entity.

Acceptance lives here, in git history — auditable, offline, and portable to any
git host. No third-party CLA service is used.
